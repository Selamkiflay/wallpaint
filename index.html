Optimal wall colors for:

bedrooms

Deep Red

In this warm yet polished bedroom bewitching red walls set a good mood. The accent pillow features a more neon shade of red that brightens up the space while still keeping it calm, cozy, and just a touch mysterious

peach

peachy color on the walls that would give the room a nice glow with the sunligh

bubblegum pink

Bright bubblegum pink is a fearless choice. In this bedroom , it asserts a youthful spirit to balance out the traditional pieces, like the dresser and tight floral patterns.

Terracotta

A large, statement piece of art helps break up the dark color. Though brown isn't exactly the most obvious paint color when decorating a bedroom, this warm nook makes a strong case for it.

kicthen

Blue on Blue

Can't decide between two shades of blue paint? Choose both! Designer Whitney Parkinson makes the case for a monochrome two-tone color palette in House Beautiful's 2022 Whole Home

Midnight Blue

designers used charcoal gray Negresco granite on the backsplash and countertops of this kitchen so they would nearly disappear against the dark paint color used on the walls, hood, and cabinets. Though it's dark navy, it appears black in certain lighting.

Olive Green and Warm Wood

Olive Green and Warm Wood allows light to shine with a bold paint color that nods to nature.

Matte Black

There's nothing cool than matte black when it comes to kitchen paint colors. Expect, that is, when you cover the bottom of the overhead cabinets a gold mirrored material.

deep brown that pairs well with neutrals and blues and provides a cozy vibe without being too boring or expected.

white sail
